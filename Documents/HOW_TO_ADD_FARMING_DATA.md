### Step 3: Update Your Profile

Go to **User Profile** and add:
- **Location**: Your actual location (e.g., "Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu")
- **Land Size**: Your land size (e.g., "10 acres")
- **Main Crop**: Your primary crop (e.g., "Rice")
- **Agriculture Experience**: Years of experience

## Quick Tips

### For Realistic Data:
1. **Seed Cost**: ₹10,000 - ₹20,000 per season
2. **Labor Cost**: ₹3,000 - ₹10,000 per season
3. **Total Cost**: ₹15,000 - ₹30,000 per season
4. **Market Price**: ₹2,000 - ₹6,000 per quintal

### For Better Analysis:
- Add records from **different years** (2021, 2022, 2023)
- Include **different seasons** (Kharif, Rabi, Summer)
- Add **various crops** (Rice, Wheat, Cotton, Sugarcane, etc.)
- Keep costs **realistic** and **complete**
- Update **prices regularly**

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ **Don't:**
- Add only 1-2 records
- Use unrealistic costs (like ₹420 total)
- Skip labor costs
- Use same crop for all records
- Forget to add year information

✅ **Do:**
- Add at least 5 records
- Use realistic costs (₹15,000-30,000)
- Include both seed and labor costs
- Vary crops and seasons
- Add data from multiple years

## Testing Your Data

After adding records, click **"🎯 Best Crop"** again. You should see:
- ✅ Higher scores (60-90/100)
- ✅ Multiple crop options
- ✅ Profit estimates
- ✅ Clear recommendations
- ✅ Meaningful insights

## Need Help?

If you're still getting poor recommendations:
1. Check if all costs are filled in
2. Verify you have at least 5 records
3. Ensure records are from different years
4. Add price data for your crops
5. Update your profile information
